TheTool - the big one!

Used for many things! Mainly monitoring for production services, generating of alerts and SMS messages for the On Duty rota etc

But also used to store and execute queries against multiple data sources, checking the statuis of MQ queues and playing messages in to Connex using Tibco. 

Original author of TheTool is Bjornar Elgsaether
